Swine influenza sLoLa

Dynamics and evolution of swine influenza in pigs

Introduction

This site provides links to resources generated as part of a BBSRC-funded strategic Longer and Larger (sLoLa) project on the dynamics and evolution of swine influenza in pigs.

Repositories

  • GenieR: an R package for the estimation of demographic trajectories from phylogenies
  • popseq: an R package for the analysis and visualisation of viral deep sequencing datasets

In progress

  • Links to viral deep sequencing datasets
  • Simulations of within-host viral evolution
